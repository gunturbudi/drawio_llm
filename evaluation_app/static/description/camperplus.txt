CamperPlus is a comprehensive platform designed to meet the needs of both parents and camp administrators. Parents can easily communicate with their child's counselors, share and cherish camp memories through photos, receive regular updates on their child's behavior and performance, and stay informed about camp events and notices. Meanwhile, camp administrators can efficiently provide feedback to parents, facilitate mass notifications, and gather valuable feedback and concerns to enhance the camp experience. CamperPlus ensures a seamless and reassuring camp experience for all involved.
