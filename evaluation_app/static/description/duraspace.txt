DuraSpace is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting open technologies that ensure long-lasting access to digital data. They collaborate with various academic, scientific, cultural, and research communities, assisting projects and services aimed at preserving collective digital heritage. Users can list collections and objects within a library unit, contact repositories, and browse collections with specific filters in the Digital Asset Management System (DAMS). DAMS managers can also track events such as object deletions for better management.
