Informed Consent

1. Project Title

Evaluation of PrivacyStory Tool

2. Contact Information

  • Primary Researcher: Guntur Budi Herwanto, University of Vienna (Email: gunturbudi@ugm.ac.id)
  • Supervisors: Gerald Quirchmayr (Email: gerald.quirchmayr@univie.ac.at) and A Min Tjoa (Email: amin@ifs.tuwien.ac.at)

3. Research Context

This PhD project explores the effectiveness of the PrivacyStory tool in aiding software professionals (developers, analysts, requirements engineers) to create privacy-focused requirements and solutions. Participation is online and involves using the tool to create privacy requirements and solutions.

4. Purpose

To evaluate the efficiency and effectiveness of PrivacyStory in generating privacy requirements and design pattern solutions.

5. Participant Selection

Your experience in software development makes you a valuable participant for assessing the tool's impact on privacy requirement generation and design solutions. Please consider your participation carefully.

6. Participation Process

  • Complete a demographic and background questionnaire.
  • Learn the Privacy-by-Design by participating in the course.
  • Use PrivacyStory to generate privacy requirements and solutions.
  • Fill out a post-use questionnaire.

7. Risks and Benefits

No known risks. Participation contributes to improving the PrivacyStory tool. Personal data collected will be basic demographic information.

8. Data Use

Data will be anonymized and used for PhD research purposes. It will be securely stored and processed to ensure confidentiality.

9. Anonymity and Confidentiality

Your personal information will be kept confidential and used only by the research team. You will be assigned a unique username for anonymity.

10. Joining the Research

If you have questions or concerns, contact gunturbudi@ugm.ac.id. To participate, please confirm your willingness after reviewing this information.
